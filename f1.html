Allow F-1 Students to Stay in the US

On Monday, July 6, 2020, Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) announced that International Students must leave the United States if their schools have opted for (or will opt for) online-only classes in the Fall 2020 semester.

Below is the petition endorsing the White House to rescind its decision to force international students back to their home countries amid a pandemic. Additionally, there are multiple links to encourage schools and local governments to support international students. Please sign your name to support this resolution.
